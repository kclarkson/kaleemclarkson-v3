Kaleem Clarkson helps leaders put people back at the center of all this change. He shows how to:

About Kaleem Clarkson

Kaleem Clarkson is a remote work advocate, people operations leader, and speaker who has spent more than a decade helping organizations design better remote employee experiences. He is the co founder and Chief Operating Officer of Blend Me, Inc, a consultancy that helps startups and small businesses become high functioning remote or hybrid workplaces.

Born in Bangor, Maine and based in Atlanta, Georgia, Kaleem is known for his practical, people centered approach to flexible work. He has been featured in Harvard Business Review, CNBC, and Fortune Magazine, named one of LinkedIn's Top Voices in Remote Work, and recognized as a top remote work advocate by major speaking bureaus. He has spoken for audiences at SXSW, HP, Visa, Make A Wish Foundation. You can find him hosting conversations about remote work on his podcasts or advising leaders on how to make flexibility work in real life.

Kaleem currently resides in Atlanta, Georgia with his family. When he is not working, you can find him mountain biking on the trails of Georgia with his headphones, blasting a little hard rock music.
